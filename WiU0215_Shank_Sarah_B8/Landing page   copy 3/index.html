WorldSurf

One Stop Shop

WorldSurf takes pride in providing individuals with exceptional service, our business caters to all types of ages and expertise, We offer training classes to individuals seeking lessons in surfing and paddle boarding. Our services range from various levels ranging from advanced to beginners we have monthly memberships and also trail classes to find out what floats you. Our surfing instructors are certified professionals qualified to teach you the basic skills of taking charge of your board and gaining the confidence to take on each wave. Water activities can be purchased year round along with the equipment necessary. WorldSurf offers a wide range of surfing equipment from: surfboards, paddle boards, skim boards, maintenance equipment, wetsuits, swim gear and much more. WorldSurf also has board rentals for surfing, paddle boarding or skim boarding along the shore. We encourage all customers to take a trail course to ensure the proper training courses have been selected. We welcome you with open waves and shining sunbeams!
